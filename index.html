Phishing Awareness Quiz

Test your ability to spot phishing attempts commonly used against UK financial services users.

How to Take the Quiz

  • You will be shown 20 realistic messages (emails, calls, texts, etc.) that could appear in a UK financial context.
  • For each message, decide quickly: Is this Phishing? or Is this Legitimate?
  • Look for red flags: urgent language, unknown senders, suspicious links, unexpected requests, mismatched branding, or too-good-to-be-true offers.
  • Questions appear in random order — there is no predictable pattern.
  • Your performance is tracked — the quiz gets harder if you do well early.
  • At the end, you’ll get your score, a grade, and personalised feedback.
